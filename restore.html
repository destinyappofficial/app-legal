Destiny - Restore & Refund Policy

Restore Purchases

If you reinstall Destiny or switch devices, you can restore previous purchases by selecting the "Restore Purchases" button in the app settings.

Refunds

Refunds are handled directly by Google Play or App Store according to their refund policies. Destiny does not process payments or issue refunds manually.

Subscription Management

You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime through your Google Play or App Store account settings.

Support

If you encounter an issue while restoring purchases, contact us at support@destinyapp.com.
